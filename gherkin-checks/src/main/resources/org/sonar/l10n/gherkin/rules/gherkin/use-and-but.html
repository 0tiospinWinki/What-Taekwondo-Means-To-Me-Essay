Use And and But instead of redundant Given/When/Then prefix to make scenarios more readable.

Noncompliant Code Example

Scenario: As a customer, add a product to the cart
  Given I am a customer
  Given I am on the catalog page           # Noncompliant
  When I add a product to my cart
  Then I should see the product in my cart

Compliant Solution

Scenario: As a customer, add a product to the cart
  Given I am a customer
  And I am on the catalog page
  When I add a product to my cart
  Then I should see the product in my cart
